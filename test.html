The Alcario and Carmen Castellano Family Collections consist of physical and digital videos, photographs, and papers that document the Castellano Family Foundation as well as the Mexican-American community around the Bay Area. These Collections are held by San Jose State University and the San Jose Public Library at the Martin Luther King, Jr. Library in San Jose, California.

        
         
         

Alcario Castellano
Video Collection

The Alcario Castellano Video Collection contains videos recorded by Castellano of events primarily based in San Jose, California and the surrounding Bay Area during the 1980s and 1990s. The collection presents various socio-cultural facets of the Mexican-American community of the time, with videos featuring events sponsored by the San Jose Chapter of the American G.I. Forum, the Chicana Latina Foundation, Cinco de Mayo parades, presentations by authors and artists, and forums and conventions. 

 

        
         
         

Photograph Collection

Papers of the Castellano Family Foundation
